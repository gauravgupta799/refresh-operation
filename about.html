About Us

To continue meeting the growing needs of swift and repetitive rehabilitation and to full-fill her passion to serve, Lisa formed the 501c3 Non-profit organization, Operation ReFresh, (ORF) February 2, 2022.

Non-Profit Organization Making Rehab Accessible to all

Consistently pleading with insurance companies to increase therapy sessions this dedicated mom vowed to her sons total rehabilitation, though told by Doctors he may never see, walk or talk, she has understood her assignment and the calling placed on her life to help others alike. With God’s guidance, self-motivation and her drive to be an advocate. She boasts with early and consistent therapy “All things are possible”, which adopted the slogans, "Failure Is not an option” and “No one owes you anything” while motivating others to never give up.

For the past 2 years Lisa has used personal funds, traveling to assist families as they go through this life altering situation, offering comfort, support and rehabilitation equipment. To date she has provided support to include rehabilitation equipment, electric wheelchairs, for easy transport and travel, as well as financial support: understanding exactly how it feels to be in this situation. Being a parent and feeling helpless. Her lone activities have inspired supporters world wide who have offered their time, money and knowledge to aid in the rehab efforts.

Lisa has built a remarkable social platform measure of trust in the ability to accomplish her vision. Because there is an expectation that she will follow through with her commitment of care and service in their best interest, there is now an opportunity to become a change agent on their behalf and to offer physical and mental rehabilitation services to trauma survivor at little or no cost is what she vows to do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for anyone impacted by brain injury, by providing support, advocacy, and information, while promoting brain injury prevention. We vow to inspire individuals into action across the world, serving in practical but consistent ways to bring forth awareness, personal development, information and social inclusion.

Our Vision

The next level vision is to expand from providing at home rehab equipment to a comprehensive in person service to include: physical, mental and personal development services.

Considering the goal to elevate the quality of life, ORF will expand to open a brick and mortar facility and offer low to no cost rehabilitation and other necessities through collaborations and direct connections.
