The Vision of a Missionary

Lisa has spent the past 2 years utilizing personal funds to offer comfort, support, and rehabilitation equipment to families going through life-altering situations.

Her dedication and drive to be an advocate, guided by God's direction and self-motivation, have led her to provide financial support and rehabilitation equipment, including electric wheelchairs for easy transport and travel. As a parent who understands the helplessness that comes with such situations, Lisa's lone activities have inspired supporters worldwide to offer their time, money, and knowledge to aid in the rehabilitation efforts.

Faces of Transformation: Inspiring Stories of Hope and Healing

A Not-for-Profit on a Mission to Make Rehab Accessible to Everyone

In a world where individuals of diverse backgrounds, genders, races, ages, and religions are often marginalized, there exists a forgotten group - those deemed in-able. Among them are individuals who endure the immense challenges brought on by traumatic brain injuries, strokes, and other life-altering circumstances. Sadly, insurance companies restrict their access to vital therapies, leaving them in dire need of support.

We fervently advocate for these individuals and their families, who tirelessly search for resources, therapies, or even a simple word of encouragement. We stand by you, affirming that you are not exempt from care and compassion. You are not alone in your journey to reintegrate into society. We are here, steadfastly committed to extending a helping hand and ensuring that your voices are heard.
